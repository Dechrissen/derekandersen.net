About me

Welcome! My name is Derek. I am a full-time graduate student studying Computational Linguistics at Stony Brook University. I made this website as a place to aggregate all of my work and interests, and to serve as a kind of archive for my projects.

My hope is that it also serves as a form of motivation for working on projects, given that it'll help me to visualize my progress. In the projects° tab, you will find a list outlining my completed projects. In the blog° tab, you will find more detailed descriptions of some of these projects, along with occasional entries on other topics. An archive of my academic papers can be found in the papers° tab.
